New, Improved, and Ready to Work!

The past two and a half years have been full of intensive and purposeful self-development, and I'm sure I will be processing the ways they've changed my life for years to come. My engagements during this time were not always directly related to my professional track, but they broadened my perspectives and skill sets, bolstered my confidence, helped me find direction, and taught me how to navigate when I'm outside my comfort zone -- all things that I think will improve my quality of work and strengthen my abilities as a teammate. Now that I'm ready to knuckle down and commit to a phase of intentional professional development, I am excited to discover my new potential in the information design and data visualization fields.
